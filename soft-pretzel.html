Soft Pretzel

Description

A delicious and easy to make soft pretzel!

Ingredients:

  • 5 ½ cups of warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 1 tablespoon of white sugar
  • 5 teaspoons of active dry yeast
  • 4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup of melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon coarse salt
  • ½ cups of baking soda
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • Coarse salt
